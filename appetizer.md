\# Garlic Bread



\## Ingredients

½ cup butter



1)1 ½ tablespoons garlic powder

2)1 tablespoon dried parsley

3)1 (1 pound) loaf Italian bread, cut into ½-inch slices

4)1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese





\## Instructions

1\. Preheat oven to 375°F.

2\. Mix butter and garlic powder and chees.

3\. Spread mixture on bread.

4\. Bake for 10 minutes.
